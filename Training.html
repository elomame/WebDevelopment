Training Your Cockatiel

Training your cockatiel can be a complex and time consuming task. However, there are a number of factors to keep in mind to give your cockatiel the best learning experience possible.

Here are some guidelines to follow when training your cockatiel.

  • Teach through repetition.
  • Some alone time with your cockatiel is recommended for bonding, but too much can be overwhelming.
  • Occasionally reward your cockatiel with treats.
  • If you're teaching your cockatiel a song, play it on repeat using an audio system of your choice. Keep the volume within reasonable levels - cockatiels have sensitive hearing.
